
# Subscription Business Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an end-to-end analytics solution for a subscription-based business, focusing on revenue performance, customer behaviour, and churn analysis.

The dashboard provides insights into key business metrics such as MRR, ARR, ARPU, and churn trends across different markets.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyse revenue growth and performance
- Understand customer behaviour and lifecycle
- Identify churn trends and retention challenges
- Compare performance across geographic regions

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Revenue shows consistent growth over time
- UK has the highest ARPU
- Customer distribution is evenly spread
- Churn is consistently high (~38–40%) across all regions

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Improve retention strategies across all markets
- Investigate causes of high churn
- Replicate UK monetisation strategies
- Reduce payment failures
